Key Takeaways

  • Use the quadratic formula to solve any quadratic equation in standard form.
  • To solve any quadratic equation, first rewrite in standard form, ax2+bx+c=0, substitute the appropriate coefficients into the quadratic formula, x=−b±b2−4ac2a, and then simplify.
